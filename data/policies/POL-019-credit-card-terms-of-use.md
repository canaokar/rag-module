# Credit Card Terms of Use

## 1. General Terms

These terms govern the use of credit cards issued by the Bank. By using the
card, the cardholder agrees to be bound by these terms.

## 2. Credit Limit

2.1 The Bank will assign a credit limit based on the cardholder's
creditworthiness. The initial limit will be communicated at the time of card
issuance.

2.2 Credit limits range from GBP 500 to GBP 25,000 for personal cards and up
to GBP 50,000 for business credit cards.

2.3 The Bank may increase the credit limit with the cardholder's consent. The
cardholder may request a decrease at any time.

## 3. Interest Rates and Fees

3.1 The standard purchase APR is 22.9% (variable). Balance transfer APR is
0% for the first 12 months, reverting to 22.9% thereafter.

3.2 Cash advance APR is 27.9% (variable). Cash advance fees are 3.0% of the
amount withdrawn, with a minimum of GBP 3.

3.3 Interest is charged on the outstanding balance from the transaction date
for cash advances and from the statement date for purchases (if the previous
balance was not paid in full).

3.4 The minimum monthly payment is the greater of: 2.5% of the outstanding
balance, GBP 25, or the total of interest and fees plus GBP 1.

3.5 A late payment fee of GBP 12 will be charged if the minimum payment is
not received by the due date shown on the statement.

## 4. Rewards Programme

4.1 The Bank's rewards credit card earns 1 point per GBP 1 spent on eligible
purchases. Bonus categories earn 2 points per GBP 1.

4.2 Points expire 36 months after the month in which they were earned.

4.3 Points may be redeemed for cashback (1 point = GBP 0.01), travel bookings,
gift cards, or charitable donations.

## 5. Security

5.1 The cardholder must take reasonable steps to keep the card and PIN secure.

5.2 Lost or stolen cards must be reported immediately by calling the 24-hour
helpline. The cardholder is not liable for unauthorised transactions reported
promptly, subject to the Payment Services Regulations 2017.

5.3 The Bank uses 3D Secure (Verified by Visa / Mastercard SecureCode) for
online transactions.

## 6. Foreign Transactions

6.1 Transactions in a foreign currency will be converted at the Mastercard or
Visa exchange rate applicable on the date of conversion, plus a non-sterling
transaction fee of 2.99%.

## 7. Closing the Account

7.1 The cardholder may close the account at any time by notifying the Bank and
repaying any outstanding balance.

7.2 The Bank may close the account or suspend the card with immediate effect
where fraud is suspected or the cardholder is in material breach of these
terms.

## 8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales.
