# Retail Lending Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the origination, underwriting, and management of retail
lending products across the Bank's personal banking portfolio. It ensures that
credit risk is taken within approved risk appetite boundaries.

## 2. Credit Approval Authority

2.1 Individual lending up to GBP 75,000 may be approved by Branch Managers.

2.2 Lending between GBP 75,001 and GBP 500,000 requires approval from the
Regional Credit Committee.

2.3 Exposures exceeding GBP 500,000 must be referred to the Group Credit
Committee with a full credit memorandum and independent risk opinion.

## 3. Underwriting Standards

3.1 Debt-to-income ratio must not exceed 42.0% for unsecured personal loans.

3.2 Loan-to-value ratio for residential mortgages must not exceed 85.0%
without mortgage insurance.

3.3 All applicants must demonstrate a minimum continuous employment history of
12 months or, for self-employed borrowers, two years of audited accounts.

3.4 Affordability assessments must include a stress test at the higher of the
product rate plus 3.0% or the Bank's standard stress rate.

## 4. Product Parameters

4.1 Maximum term for unsecured personal loans is 7 years.

4.2 Maximum term for residential mortgages is 35 years, subject to the borrower
being no older than 70 at the end of the mortgage term.

4.3 Interest-only mortgages require a credible repayment strategy with verified
assets of at least 50% of the outstanding balance.

## 5. Portfolio Monitoring

5.1 The retail credit portfolio must be reviewed monthly, with concentration
limits monitored against the approved Risk Appetite Statement.

5.2 Early warning indicators must be reported to the Chief Risk Officer within
3 business days of detection.

5.3 Arrears management procedures must be initiated when a borrower is 30 days
past due on any scheduled payment.

## 6. Governance and Oversight

The Board of Directors retains ultimate responsibility for the matters covered
by this document. Day-to-day oversight is delegated to the relevant Executive
Committee member as specified in the Bank's Governance Map.

The policy owner must present a summary of compliance status, key metrics, and
emerging risks to the relevant Board sub-committee at least semi-annually.

Any material exceptions to this policy must be approved by the relevant
Executive Committee member and recorded in the policy exception register.
Exceptions are valid for a maximum period of 12 months and must be reviewed
before renewal.

## 7. Review and Amendment

This document must be reviewed at least annually or following a material change
in regulation, business strategy, or risk profile. The review must be documented
and signed off by the policy owner.

Amendments must follow the Bank's Document Change Control Procedure. All staff
affected by changes must be notified within 15 business days and must acknowledge
receipt.

## 8. Related Documents

This document should be read in conjunction with the Bank's Risk Appetite
Statement, the Credit Risk Policy, the Mortgage Underwriting Policy, and the
Consumer Duty Implementation Plan.
