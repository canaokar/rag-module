# Business Account General Terms

## 1. General Terms

These terms and conditions govern the provision of business banking products
and services by the Bank. By opening a business account, the account holder
agrees to be bound by these terms.

## 2. Eligibility

2.1 Business accounts are available to sole traders, partnerships, limited
companies, limited liability partnerships, and other eligible legal entities
registered in the United Kingdom.

2.2 All directors, partners, or beneficial owners holding 25% or more of the
business must complete the Bank's identification and verification process.

2.3 The business must have an annual turnover of at least GBP 10,000 for
standard business accounts or at least GBP 1,000,000 for premium business
accounts.

## 3. Fees and Charges

3.1 The standard business account has no monthly fee for the first 18 months
from account opening. Thereafter, a monthly fee of GBP 7.50 applies.

3.2 Transaction fees:

- Electronic payments (Faster Payments, BACS): GBP 0.25 per transaction
- Cash deposits: 0.70% of the amount deposited (minimum GBP 1.00)
- Cash withdrawals: GBP 0.65 per GBP 100 withdrawn
- International payments: GBP 15 per transaction plus applicable FX margin

3.3 The premium business account has a monthly fee of GBP 25 and includes
75 free electronic transactions per month and preferential international
payment rates.

## 4. Business Overdraft

4.1 Business overdraft facilities are available subject to credit assessment.

4.2 Arranged overdraft interest is charged at the Bank's base rate plus 5.5%
(variable). Unarranged overdraft usage is charged at the Bank's base rate
plus 15.0%.

4.3 An annual facility fee of 1.5% of the agreed overdraft limit applies.

4.4 Overdraft limits are reviewed annually. The Bank may reduce or withdraw
the facility with 30 business days notice.

## 5. Authorised Signatories

5.1 The account holder must designate at least one authorised signatory.

5.2 For transactions exceeding GBP 25,000, dual authorisation is required
unless a single signatory mandate has been expressly agreed.

5.3 Changes to authorised signatories must be notified to the Bank in writing
and will take effect within 5 business days.

## 6. Statements and Reporting

6.1 Monthly statements are provided electronically at no charge. Paper
statements are available at GBP 2.50 per statement.

6.2 Transaction data is available via the Business Banking API in accordance
with Open Banking standards.

## 7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Disputes will be
subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.
