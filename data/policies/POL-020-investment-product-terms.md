# Investment Product Terms

## 1. General Terms

These terms and conditions govern the Bank's investment products, including
Stocks and Shares ISAs, General Investment Accounts, and the Bank's managed
portfolio service. By opening an investment account, the customer agrees to be
bound by these terms.

## 2. Eligibility

2.1 Investment accounts may be opened by individuals aged 18 or over who are
resident in the United Kingdom for tax purposes.

2.2 Stocks and Shares ISAs are subject to the annual ISA subscription limit
set by HMRC (currently GBP 20,000 per tax year).

2.3 The minimum investment is GBP 100 for lump sum contributions and GBP 25
per month for regular investment plans.

## 3. Risk Warnings

3.1 The value of investments can go down as well as up, and the customer may
get back less than they invest.

3.2 Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results.

3.3 The Bank does not provide personal investment advice. Customers seeking
advice should consult an independent financial adviser.

3.4 Investments held in a Stocks and Shares ISA or General Investment Account
are not protected by the FSCS deposit protection scheme. However, the FSCS
investment protection scheme may apply, covering up to GBP 85,000 per person
per firm.

## 4. Charges

4.1 Platform fee: 0.25% per annum on the total value of investments held,
calculated daily and charged monthly.

4.2 Fund charges: ongoing charges figures (OCFs) vary by fund and are
disclosed in the relevant Key Investor Information Document (KIID) or Key
Information Document (KID).

4.3 Dealing charges: GBP 7.99 per trade for share dealing. Fund purchases and
sales are free of dealing charges.

4.4 There is no charge for transferring investments to another provider.

## 5. Available Investments

5.1 Customers may invest in the following asset classes through the Bank's
platform: UK and international equities, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), unit
trusts, open-ended investment companies (OEICs), investment trusts, and
government bonds.

5.2 The managed portfolio service offers five risk-rated portfolios ranging
from Cautious (Portfolio 1) to Adventurous (Portfolio 5).

## 6. Custody and Settlement

6.1 Investments are held in the Bank's nominee account on behalf of the
customer. The customer retains beneficial ownership at all times.

6.2 Share transactions settle on a T+1 basis (one business day after the
trade date).

6.3 Fund transactions are typically settled within 3-5 business days.

## 7. Withdrawals

7.1 Withdrawal requests for cash balances are processed within 2 business
days.

7.2 Where investments must be sold to fund a withdrawal, the withdrawal will
be processed within 5 business days of the sale settling.

## 8. Tax Treatment

8.1 Stocks and Shares ISA: income and capital gains are free from UK income
tax and capital gains tax.

8.2 General Investment Account: the customer is responsible for reporting and
paying any applicable income tax, capital gains tax, or dividend tax.

## 9. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales.
